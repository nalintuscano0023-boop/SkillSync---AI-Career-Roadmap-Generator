🎯 SkillSync – AI Career Roadmap Generator

Transform your tech career aspirations into a personalized, actionable learning journey powered by AI




🌟 Overview

SkillSync is an intelligent career development platform that uses AI to generate personalized tech career roadmaps. Whether you're a beginner starting your programming journey or an intermediate developer looking to upskill, SkillSync analyzes your current skills and creates a data driven learning path to land your dream tech job.

Why SkillSync?

🚀 Career Success Gap: 73% of tech professionals struggle to identify what skills they need to advance
📊 Skill Value Loss: Learners spend 40% more time on irrelevant skills without proper guidance
⏱️ Time Constraints: Building your own roadmap takes weeks; SkillSync generates one in minutes
